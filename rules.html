Game Rules

  • Two players take turns placing X or O on the board.
  • The goal is to get a straight line of X/O horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • The number of marks in a row needed to win equals the board size (example: 3 in a row for 3x3, 4 for 4x4).
  • If the board fills up without a winner, the game is a draw.
  • You can play against another player or the computer with three difficulty levels.
  • Choose your board size from 3x3.
